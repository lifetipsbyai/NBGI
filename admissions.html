Admission Requirements

National Diploma (ND) Programs

  • SSCE/WAEC with at least 5 credits including Mathematics and English
  • Minimum of 2 years post-secondary education
  • Relevant science subjects for chosen program

Higher National Diploma (HND) Programs

  • Relevant National Diploma (ND) certificate
  • Minimum of Lower Credit grade
  • One year post-ND industrial experience (preferred)

Certificate Programs

  • Minimum of SSCE/WAEC certificate
  • Basic computer literacy (for tech programs)
  • Passion for learning and career advancement

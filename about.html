Our Journey

A timeline of our growth and achievements in STEM education.

2018

Foundation

Noble Genius Institute of Science and Technology was founded by Gilbert Chidiebere Nnamdi with a vision to revolutionize STEM education in Nigeria.

2019

First Graduation

Celebrated our first graduating class with 95% employment rate within 6 months of graduation.

2021

Research Excellence

Established state-of-the-art research facilities and launched breakthrough projects in biotechnology and artificial intelligence.

2023

Industry Partnerships

Forged strategic partnerships with leading technology companies, creating internship and job placement opportunities for our students.

2024

Expansion & Growth

Launched new certificate programs in emerging technologies and expanded our campus facilities to accommodate growing student enrollment.

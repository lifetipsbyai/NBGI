Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions.

Admission requirements vary by program. For National Diploma programs, you need SSCE/WAEC with at least 5 credits including Mathematics and English. For HND programs, you need a relevant ND certificate. Certificate programs require SSCE/WAEC certificate.

Tuition fees vary by program: National Diploma (₦250,000/year), Higher National Diploma (₦300,000/year), and Certificate Programs (₦150,000/program). We also offer scholarships and financial aid options.

Yes! We offer merit-based scholarships up to 50% tuition waiver for outstanding students, need-based financial aid, and industry-sponsored scholarships. Applications close June 30th each year.

We offer National Diploma and Higher National Diploma programs in Computer Science, Biotechnology, Cybersecurity, and Software Engineering. We also have certificate programs in AI & Robotics, Data Science, and Digital Marketing.

Our academic year begins in October. We have two intakes: September (main intake) and January (limited programs). Applications open in March and close in August.
